The Three Pillars of Multivitamin Quality

Pillar 1: Bioavailability (The "Form" Factor)

The human body must convert many vitamins into an active form. The best brands bypass this conversion entirely. Look for:

  • **B Vitamins:** Use active forms like **Methylfolate** and **P-5-P** (instead of Folic Acid and Pyridoxine HCl).
  • **Minerals:** Use highly absorbent **Chelated** forms (Glycinate, Picolinate) instead of inexpensive Oxides.
