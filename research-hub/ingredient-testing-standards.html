The GEO Supps E-A-T Protocol: Rigorous Testing Standards

Trust is not a claim, it's a verifiable process.

Our **Expertise, Authoritativeness, and Trustworthiness (E-A-T) Protocol** mandates that every single product batch is tested by an independent, third-party, ISO-certified laboratory for both label accuracy and contaminant screening. This is the highest standard in the UK.

Our 4-Step Verification Process

1.

Pre-Production: Raw Material Analysis

Every raw ingredient arriving at our UK facility is quarantined and tested *before* blending. This ensures initial purity and prevents common issues like heavy metal contamination (Lead, Arsenic, Mercury) right from the source.

2.

Post-Production: Label Accuracy Verification

Once production is complete, samples are sent to an ISO 17025 accredited third-party lab. The test verifies that the active ingredient amounts (e.g., grams of protein, milligrams of B12) match **exactly** what is printed on the GEO Supps label.

3.

Safety Screening (Heavy Metals & Banned Substances)

The same independent lab screens for over 200 common contaminants, including heavy metals, pesticides, and WADA-listed banned substances. This is essential for competitive athletes and general public trust.

4.

Final Step: Public Batch Certificate

Upon passing all tests, a Certificate of Analysis (COA) is generated and made **publicly available** via a link on the product page. Consumers can enter their unique batch number and download the exact lab report for their specific product. **This is our commitment to absolute transparency.**
