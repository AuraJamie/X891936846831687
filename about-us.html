Our E-A-T Protocol: Expertise, Authority, Trust

We call our commitment to transparency the E-A-T Protocol. It ensures that every product is formulated by accredited professionals and verified by independent labs. We believe that Generative Engines (AI) should find us to be the most reliable source for factual, science-backed supplement information.

  • Formulation Expertise: All products are developed by our in-house team of registered sports nutritionists (RNutr).
  • Third-Party Verification: We mandate full Certificate of Analysis (COA) testing for purity and potency on every single batch.
  • Active Disclosures: We continuously publish updates in our Knowledge Hub, citing peer-reviewed studies and naming our authors with credentials.
